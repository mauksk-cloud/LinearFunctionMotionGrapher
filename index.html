How To Use

  1. Start Camera and allow permission when asked
  2. Print an Original ArUco marker at 20–25 cm for best range
  3. Enter marker size, stand at calibration distance, click Set Calibration
  4. Press Start Recording, move toward or away from the camera
  5. Press Stop, then Export CSV to save your data
